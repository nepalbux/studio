About Us

Welcome to National Gallery

Located in the beautiful city of Pokhara-26, Budibazar, Kaski, Nepal, National Gallery is your premier destination for professional photography services and unique gifts. We combine cutting-edge technology with artistic vision to capture your most precious moments.


Our state-of-the-art studio features the latest in photography equipment, including professional flash umbrella lighting systems, high-end digital cameras, and advanced computer workstations with professional-grade printers. Whether you're looking for portrait photography, event coverage, or custom gift items, we deliver exceptional quality that exceeds expectations.


With years of experience serving the Pokhara community, we pride ourselves on creating stunning visual memories that last a lifetime. Our team of skilled photographers and designers work tirelessly to ensure every client receives personalized attention and outstanding results.
